Privacy Policy

Streamer Clips Farm is a personal content publishing tool used to prepare and upload short-form videos to TikTok and other social media platforms.

The app may request access to basic TikTok account information and video publishing permissions only after the account owner authorizes it.

The app uses the granted permissions only to publish videos selected by the account owner.

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with advertisers or third parties.

Access tokens and account configuration are stored locally by the account owner and are used only for authorized publishing actions.

The app does not collect payment information, location data, contacts, private messages, or sensitive personal information.

If you want to request deletion of any data related to this app, contact us at: 8gogle@gmail.com
